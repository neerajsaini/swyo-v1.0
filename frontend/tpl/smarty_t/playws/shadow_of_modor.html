CONTROLS
  • General keys
    A,W,S,D - movement
    E - Drain orc for arrows
    Space - sprint/jump/dodge
    Shift - stealth
    left mouse - hit
    right mouse - parry
  • Bow
    left mouse (hold) - bow mode
    right mouse (hold) - charge arrow
  • Special moves
    Shift + left click - stealth kill
    Bow (aim on enemy) + X - blink kill
    ......
    Experiment for additional special moves
